Juan, The Money Boss!

Puts you back in controle.

"Juan, The Money Boss" Mobile Application is the new personal financial adviser that everyone needs. This App which will be offered by your bank will assist you with wise advice on how to control your financial life. Money is no longer the boss, you are.

Project Team Members

App's Features

This App will offer the following:

  1. Give you an over all visual presentation of your monthly spending pattern
  2. Advice on how you can save money based on the spending category and the surrounding conditions (weather, discount offers, alternative solutions, etc).
Example Scenarios:
  • Food: You spent 350$ on Mexican Restaurants.
    Watson Burritos & Taco's recipe can be prepared easily from fresh ingredients for 20% of the cost and %200 of the taste.
  • Transportation: You spent 450$ on gas this month.
    Weather expectations for this week looks awesome, consider riding your bicycle to work. If you take your bicycle to work twice a week you could save 3000$ on gas a year.
  • Entertainment: You spent 150$ on watching Action movies at AMC.
    AMC has a membership program that can save you 10% on your tickets. Consider joining their program.
